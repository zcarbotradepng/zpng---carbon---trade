ZPNG Carbon Trade Facilitator

The ZPNG Carbon Trade Facilitator is an initiative by Leuka Roger Kehenae, aiming to connect landowners in Papua New Guinea with international carbon trade markets.

About the Project

The project is based at Zarinka in the Dunatina LLG of Henganofi District, Eastern Highlands Province. It focuses on forest conservation, carbon offset programs, and sustainable native tree replanting through the long-established Korente Native Plant Nursery.

Our Mission

To promote climate action, empower indigenous communities, and facilitate fair and transparent access to global carbon trading opportunities.
